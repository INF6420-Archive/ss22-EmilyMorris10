Sugar Cookies

Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Bake cookie dough balls for 10 minutes.
2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
½ teaspoon baking powder
1 cup butter, softened
1 ½ cups white sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Dog Biscuits

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Bake cut-out cookie shapes 40 minutes.
2 large eggs
½ cup canned pumpkin
2 tablespoons peanut butter
½ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon water, or more if necessary

Red Velvet Cookies

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Bake slightly flattened cookie dough balls for 10 minutes.
Red velvet cake mix - make sure it is the 15.25 oz size.
Eggs - room temperature eggs.
Oil - Canola or Vegetable oil.
Powdered sugar

Peanut Butter Cookies

Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Mix wet ingredients first, then combine dry ingredients.
Bake cookie dough balls for 10 minutes.
1 cup unsalted butter
1 cup crunchy peanut butter
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 large eggs eggs
2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
½ teaspoon salt
1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
